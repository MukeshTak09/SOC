    SWAGGERS OUTBREAK CREW

    About SOC

    Swaggers Outbreak Crew is an united group of creative and passionate dancers to take dance skills to the next level.

    Our Journey

    SOC was started in 2011 by enthusiastic seniors of BKBIET to redefine the dance culture in the college.

    Winnings

    As said, Hardwork always pays off. Starting 2011 to 2016, continuously SOC secured 1st champion rank in BASANT group dance competition. From 2011-2016, 2018 SOC was undefeated champion.

    DANCE

    A min of dance act needs months practice, synchronization, Rhythm and Love. The best thing about being a dance group is there is nothing we can't do, we all need just practice.

    Family

    We Dance, we Love, we Smile, we Cry, we Win and we Lose together. SOC is a Family more than a dance crew. The biggest achievement for us is our valuable, trustworthy and supercool team whehter it is on stage or off the stage.
